Freshness & Storage 🧁

Cookies & bars: best within 4 days at room temp in an airtight container, or freeze up to 2 months.
Cakes with buttercream: refrigerate if not eaten within 24 hours; let sit at room temp ~20 min before serving.
Peach cobbler: refrigerate after 2 hours out; warm gently before eating.
Always keep away from direct sunlight and heat, and reseal the box tightly between servings.
